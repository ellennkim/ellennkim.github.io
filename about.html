about

Hello, I'm Ellen!

I am an undergraduate student at Northwestern pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Neuroscience.

My current academic journey is fueled by a curiosity for the mind and a passion for the intersection of creation and technology. As an aspiring software engineer, I am on a quest to blend these two realms and create innovative, optimized technological solutions using artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Over the past few years, I have delved into academia through hands-on experiences in various neuroscience labs, such as the Weinshenker Lab @ Emory and the Ann Kennedy Lab @ Northwestern's Feinberg. These experiences have allowed me to hone analytical and problem-solving skills while cultivating my interests in behavioral and computational neuroscience. As I transition to the next phase of my academic and professional growth, I am eager to apply this foundation to real-world challenges by leveraging my technical skillset and my neuroscience knowledge.

Outside of my academic commitments, I love exploring new cafes, sewing new items to add to my closet, and recharging with contemporary R&B music.

If you're interested in staying updated on this journey, please feel free to contact me!
